How to export your emails from Yahoo Mail

  1. Go to Yahoo Mail Settings
  2. Click "More Settings"
  3. Select "Mailboxes"
  4. Click "Export" to download your MBOX file

Your data stays on your computer. No emails are uploaded to any server.
